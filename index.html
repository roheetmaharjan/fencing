Welcome

Re-Create Fencing specialise in the supply and installation of superior quality timber fencing. We are owner operated, the person that quotes the fence builds the fence. We aim to exceed your expectations, our professional and friendly fencers will complete your fence to your specifications. We deliver what we promise, on time every time. We pride ourselves on our quality building materials and workmanship, providing exceptional customer service and fences that stand the test of time. We are a locally owned business servicing the Bass Coast region; Cranbourne, Wonthaggi, Inverloch, Corinella, Coronet Bay, Koo Wee Rup, Bass, Phillip Island and surrounds.
